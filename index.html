Connect with fellow alumni at J. Liu’s
 

THE OHIO STATE UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

  
 

Your college, your city! Connect with the College of Engineering and fellow alumni for a new series of unique, local experiences with an inside scoop on the college happenings.

Columbus won the U.S. Department of Transportation’s Smart City Challenge. Hear from Carla Bailo, assistant vice president for mobility research and business development, and Joanna Pinkerton, co-director of the Honda/OSU partnership, about what this means for Cbus and the college’s role in shaping the future of transportation. As an added bonus, meet Jason Liu, owner of the J. Liu restaurants in Worthington and Dublin and proud Engineering alum! Heavy appetizers and drinks provided.

Buckeyes Connect in the Smart City

Tuesday, March 7, 2017 • 6 – 8 p.m.

J. Liu of Worthington
6880 N. High St., Worthington, OH 43085

Register by February 28 at go.osu.edu/cbus-smartcity
